About Connect Pantry

Connect Pantry helps neighborhood food agencies plan smarter with the community they serve. Clients anonymously share what categories they expect to need each week; staff review demand trends, set supply levels, and prepare Monday Food Lifeline orders; and neighborhood donors can pledge to fill remaining gaps. No complicated inventory systems or integrations — just technology, food, and community working together to connect needs with impact.
